📌 What is GreenLume?

GreenLume is a personal sustainability habit tracker that transforms abstract environmental goals into a clear, measurable daily practice. It bridges the gap between good intentions and consistent action — giving users the structure, feedback, and motivation they need to build lasting green habits.

The core insight: people don't lack awareness about sustainability. They lack a system. GreenLume is that system.

🎯 The Problem We Solve

Most people who care about sustainability fall into one of these traps:
- Using generic habit apps with no environmental context
- Following social media advice with no accountability mechanism
- Journaling or spreadsheet tracking that collapses under a busy week

None of these close the loop between **action and impact**. GreenLume does.

👤 Who It's For

Primary User — The Intentional Beginner
- Age 18–35, mobile-native, urban or semi-urban
- Already cares about sustainability but lacks a system
- Familiar with habit/wellness tracking (fitness, sleep, mindfulness)
- Wants sustainability as a personal identity, not just occasional behaviour

✨ Core Features (MVP)

 🗂️ Daily Action Menu
- 15–20 trackable green habits across **5 categories:**
  - 🔋 Energy · 🗑️ Waste · 🚌 Transport · 🥗 Food · 💧 Water
- One-tap logging — completable in under 30 seconds
- Custom action creation for personal habits not in the default menu

🏆 Points & Progression
- Points calibrated to **real environmental impact (EPA & UNEP data)
- 5-tier level system: Seedling → Sprout → Sapling → Tree → Forest
- Streak tracker for consecutive days logging 3+ actions

🏅 Badge System
Milestones worth celebrating:
- 7-Day Streak
- Plastic-Free Week
- Public Transport Champion
- 30 Meatless Meals

📊 Impact Dashboard
Cumulative environmental contribution displayed in human-scale terms:
- CO₂ saved
- Plastic items avoided
- Water conserved

🎯 Weekly Challenges
Bonus point challenges to drive re-engagement and habit diversity.

📅 Monthly Impact Report
A summary of the user's full environmental contribution across all categories.

🚫 Out of Scope (MVP)

These are deferred until post product-market fit:
- Social features (leaderboards, friend challenges, community feeds)
- AI-powered personalization or chatbot interactions
- Third-party integrations (fitness trackers, smart home devices)
- Elaborate UI customization (themes, avatars, profile personalization)

🎨 Design Principles

The product should feel like a premium wellness app, not a game.

| Principle                     | What it means |
|-----------              |--------------|
| Speed over depth | No critical interaction should require more than 2 taps |
| Honesty over flattery | All impact data must be accurate and citable — never inflated |
| Calm over noise | Every UI element serves the user, not competes for attention |

Visual direction: Deep greens, warm neutrals, natural earth tones. Legible, trustworthy typography. Gamification that feels earned, not cheap.

⚠️ Key Risks

- The Week One Cliff — most habit apps lose users in the first 7 days. Onboarding must be engineered to survive this window
- Impact Data Credibility — all figures trace back to EPA / UNEP sources. No inflation, no estimation
- Gamification Fatigue — rewards must feel meaningful, not mechanical
- Scope Creep — the team is a disciplined gatekeeper of the MVP boundary
